Cancellation Policy

In order to book your date and time, we require a 100% of the invoice paid.  If  you cancel your event and give us a notice with at least 24 hours in advance, we refund 50% of the total invoiced or we can reschedule for new available date on the same calendar year. If we are not available the date and time you request for rescheduling, we will do the 50% refund. *Cancellation Notice must be in writing. Email to   *Our cancellation policy is structured this way because once you book that date and time, the artist becomes unavailable to work another event but we commit to pay them for the job.

Inclement Weather Policy

In the event of rain, we can reschedule for another date available on the same year if we receive notice with at least 24 hours in advance.  In the event of unpredictable weather conditions like tornado, flood, icy roads, hurricane, etc. we can reschedule for another date of the same year but no refund will be issued.

Important Policy

Please provide reasonable accommodations for the artist(s). This includes but is not limited to shade from sun(we provide our own tent if requested), under cover for rain.  This will also benefit your guests as they like to watch or wait in line. Keep in mind that our paint do not do well under extreme conditions.

Please Provide at least one adult to supervise children that are waiting to be painted and  to cut the line upon the artist's request near the end of the last hour. If the child or adult has flu or cold symptoms, we will be more than happy to paint them but  only on the arm.

It is the parents responsibility to inform the artist of any/all skin allergies and sensitivities.  All of our products are 100% hypoallergenic but we are not responsible for any allergic reactions  that might occur.

Hygiene and safety is one of our main priorities. We clean and sanitize our equipment before and after each event.  Use only FDA approved cosmetic quality face and body paints as well as glitter and glue for our gems.

We use fun things like glitter and gems. Strokes Of Faith and affiliates  are not responsible for  any injury they might cause. Ig: Improper cleaning or rubbing of the face painting design or Jewels & glitter designs. Plastic gems or other accessories may be a chocking hazard for a child.
